🐖 Pig Game Rules

On a turn a player rolls the dice repeatedly. The goal is to accumulate as many points as possible, adding up the numbers rolled on the dice. However, if the players rolls a 1, the player's turn is over and any points accumulated during this round are lost. The player can choose to hold the currently accumulated points or to continue rolling for higher score. When a player reaches 100 points, the game is over and that player is the winner.
